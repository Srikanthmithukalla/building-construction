ABOUT

Building construction is the process of adding structures to real property, commonly involving various stages from planning to completion. It begins with the design phase, where architects and engineers create detailed blueprints that meet client requirements and local building codes.After the foundation is set, the structural framework is constructed, often using steel, wood, or concrete, depending on the project’s scale and design. This framework provides the skeleton of the building.Finishing touches include installing doors, windows, insulation, and interior fixtures like lighting, flooring, and paint. The final inspection ensures that the building meets safety regulations before it is ready for occupancy. Throughout the process, construction teams must coordinate to manage time, costs, and materials efficiently, while adhering to safety standards and environmental considerations.
